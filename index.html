Where comfort & technology sleep

Your smart mattress shouldn't play dumb when it comes to sleep. Similar products focus too heavily on technology, letting the construction of the mattress fall by the wayside.

Matrix sleep sensor technology is embedded into plush, high-performance memory foam, creating a mattress that is as comfortable as it is intelligent.

After all, you want a mattress that is also smart, not a piece of tech that you can also sleep on.

Beauty and brains, made in the USA

Cooling Fabric

The innovative fabric cover is cool to the touch and wicks away moisture to speed up recovery and regulate temperature.

Eco Foam Zone

Our plant-based, eco-friendly memory foam delivers superior comfort and durability.

Plitech Foam Zone

An extra layer of memory foam bridges the top and bottom layers, stabilizing the body and ensuring optimum support.

Core Foam Zone

The foundation of the mattress. Formed with a low-density flex urethane foam that keeps the mattress lighter and stronger.
